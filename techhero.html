Tech Hero Profile: Joy Buolamwini

Joy Buolamwini

Short Bio: Joy Buolamwini is a computer scientist and AI researcher who founded the Algorithmic Justice League to fight bias in artificial intelligence. As a Black woman in tech, Buolamwini has exposed racial and gender bias in AI systems, particularly in facial recognition software.

Key Contributions:

  • Founded the Algorithmic Justice League, advocating for fair and inclusive AI systems.
  • Exposed racial and gender biases in facial recognition software through her "Gender Shades" project.
  • Advocates for diverse representation in tech and AI development.

Notable Recognition:

Buolamwini's work has been featured in major publications like The New York Times and The Guardian, and she has been named to TIME 100 Next and Forbes 30 Under 30 in Science.
